# Chapter 1
## Vision

The Admin Platform is the mobile command center of AAA Infinity AI.

Administrators can securely monitor, configure and manage the entire platform from an Android device.

Ari AI assists administrators with analysis, monitoring and recommendations while critical actions remain under administrator control.
