# Ari OS Kernel

Version: 3.0

# 1. Purpose

Ari OS Kernel is the central intelligence layer of AAA Infinity AI.

Every request, event, workflow, automation, notification and AI action passes through Ari.

The Android application never communicates directly with AI providers.
